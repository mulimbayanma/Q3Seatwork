GINNY & GEORGIA

SYNOPSIS:

In the New England town of Wellsbury, where Georgia wishes to settle down with her daughter Ginny and son Austin to offer them a better life than she had, Ginny Miller, a fifteen-year-old who is more mature than her thirty-year-old mother, Georgia, is the main character of Ginny & Georgia.

CHARACTERS:

Virginia "Ginny" Miller




Virginia "Ginny" Miller is a 16-year-old teenager who lives in Wellsbury, Massachusetts. She is the daughter of Georgia Miller
and Zion Miller and the older maternal half-sister of Austin.







Georgia Miller




Georgia Miller is a 30-year-old and is the
mother of Ginny and Austin Miller. She decides to settle down with them
in Wellsbury to give them a better life than she had.







Austin Miller




Austin Miller is a 9-year-old who lives in Wellsbury, Massachusetts.
He is the son of Georgia Miller, as well as the younger half-brother of Ginny Miller.







Zion Miller



Zion Miller is a 33-year-old living in Boston
away from his family. He is the father of Ginny Miller.
Him and Georgia Miller never married but she decided to use his last name.







Paul Randolph




Paul Randolph is in his mid-30s and he is currently the husband
of Georgia Miller. Paul is the mayor of Wellsbury, Massachusetts.
